I'm a senior product designer who drives clarity and innovation through experimentation, accessibility, and technical fluency.

I help teams test ideas quickly, reduce ambiguity, and create scalable solutions grounded in simplicity and purpose. My approach is centered around strategic thinking and collaboration—with a focus on making design more effective, sustainable, and human.

Experience

Product Design

  • nib Senior Product Designer 2023 - Present

    Lead designer in the Sales team, leading experimentation and accessibility initiatives company wide.

  • Greater Bank UX Designer 2019 - 2023

    Designer on payments and messaging. Lead design on iOS and Android, establishing mobile patterns.

  • University Of Newcastle UX/UI Designer 2017 - 2019

    While completing a Bachelor of Visual Communication and Design (with Distinction), I worked on several internal UX projects—redesigning the student portal and improving an internal SharePoint site for staff. I also mentored new students, supporting their transition into university life.

  • Great Southern Electrical Electrical Technician 2015 - 2017

    Worked as a qualified electrical technician across new infrastructure projects and maintenance contracts, including leading a team on a subdivision build and managing electrical systems for Steggles in Griffith. Also handled emergency repair work and day-to-day operations in the field.

  • Essential Energy Electrical Apprentice / Linesman 2009 - 2015

    Completed my electrical apprenticeship and became a qualified linesman, working on the construction, repair, and maintenance of overhead and underground networks.

How I work with others

I care about the people behind the work and how teams work together. I actively mentor designers and developers, and look for ways to build shared understanding across roles.

Whether it’s leading workshops, helping shape direction, or just being someone others can rely on, I aim to create an environment where people feel capable, and supported.

Outside of work

I enjoy spending time with my wife and our miniature schnauzer, Dolly. We’re often out hiking, cooking Japanese food at home, playing board games with friends, or heading to the movies.

I’ve always liked making things—lately that’s included experimenting with 3D modelling and learning React—but I try to keep a good balance between exploring new tools and switching off completely.

Luke Ylias

Senior Product Designer exploring the joys of creating in both
digital and physical spaces.

I like building things that help people.

I’ve always enjoyed building things — whether it’s designing interfaces, writing code, or setting up experiments to see what works. That mix of creativity and logic is what keeps me interested.

Over time, I’ve gravitated toward areas like accessibility, front-end prototyping, and experimentation. Letting me make tangible, meaningful improvements to how things work.

Prior to being a designer I worked in the electrical industry for a decade.

I care about the people behind the work.

I try to be the kind of teammate who pays attention — to how others work, what they need, and where I can be useful. Everyone brings something different, and I think it’s worth taking the time to understand that.

I enjoy mentoring designers and developers, and I try to support people whether they’re in my immediate team or not. That might mean sharing what I know, helping someone think through a problem, or just being someone they can rely on. And when the work’s going well, it should be enjoyable too.

A bit about me

Outside of design, I’m into music, games, and spending time with my wife and our mini schnauzer, Dolly. I grew up on video games and hip-hop, and still enjoy both.

Lately, I’ve been learning React and experimenting with 3D modelling.
